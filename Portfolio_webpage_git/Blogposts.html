Machine Learning Mastery - Batch & Epoch

The Machine Learning Mastery article explains the concepts of batches and epochs in the context of training neural networks with stochastic gradient descent. It clarifies that a batch refers to the number of samples processed before the model is updated, whereas an epoch is the number of complete passes through the entire training dataset. This article helped me to understand how models learn and was instrumental whilst building my first deep learning algorithm.
