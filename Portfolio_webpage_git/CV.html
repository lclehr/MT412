Biography

After finishing my high school diploma at Lessing Gymnasium in Frankfurt, I embarked on a transformative journey to volunteer in Malawi for one year. After seeing China's impressive influence in Africa while volunteering, I initially aspired to pursue a German-Chinese double degree at ESB Business School in Reutlingen. My goal was to seamlessly merge language and business studies, whilst exploring a new culture. However, the tides of a global pandemic redirected my journey towards the Emerald Isle, where I am now pursuing a Double Degree at Dublin City University.
